Demo scene designed for HDRP, but you can also use the model resources in compatible with Legacy PBR shader scene. Materials and textures included, just apply them to prefabs.

Naming:
Textures - prefix of "TX_"
Static Meshes - prefix of "SM_"
Skeletal Meshes - prefix of "SK_"
Prefabs - prefix of "FB_"
Materials(Standard) - prefix of "MS_"
Materials(HDRP) - prefix of "MH_"
Animator Controller - suffix of "_AnimC"
